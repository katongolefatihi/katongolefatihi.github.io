Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, and custom furniture.

Do you deliver outside Kampala?
Yes! We deliver nationwide across Uganda. Delivery within Kampala is typically a flat fee, while upcountry deliveries are calculated based on the distance and the size of your order. Please contact us on WhatsApp for an exact quote to your district.
Do you require a deposit before starting work?
For all custom orders and bulk institutional furniture (like schools or pharmacies), we require a 60% commitment deposit to begin purchasing raw materials. The remaining 40% is paid upon delivery and your full satisfaction.
Can I request a custom design?
Absolutely. We have skilled carpenters who can bring your vision to life. If you have a picture of a sofa, desk, or cabinet you saw online, send it to our WhatsApp and we will give you a quotation to build it locally.
What kind of wood do you use?
We pride ourselves on durability. The majority of our premium furniture is crafted using treated Ugandan Mahogany and Mvule, ensuring it resists termites and lasts for generations.
